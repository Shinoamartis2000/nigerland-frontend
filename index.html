Our Company

Our Story

Nigerland was established in 2000 under the leadership of Professor Francis Ngwaba, an American Fulbright Scholar and Professor of English Literature.

We have partnered with tertiary institutions to train over 8,000 people who have contributed to the growth of the Nigerian economy. Our clients include The Polytechnic Calabar, Grace Polytechnic Surulere, Abia State University, Nigerian Airspace Management Agency, and Federal Ministry of Mines and Steel Development.

Mission & Vision

Our underlying philosophy is the betterment of man and his society. We believe that if man is properly developed, then mankind can progress.

Mission: To develop human capital through innovative training, quality publications, and impactful social initiatives.

Vision: To be Africa's leading consulting consortium for human capital development and social transformation.

Nigerland Books Foundation

Promoting literacy, ethics, and values for a better society

Building a Better Society Through Education

Nigerland Books Foundation (NBF) is a knowledge-based NGO that seeks to instill Morals, Ethics and Values (MEVs) which are largely the bane of development.

We aim to eradicate illiteracy, promote reading culture, and use education as a tool to fight hunger and ignorance. Through our initiatives, we donate books to schools, organize reading programs, and promote positive thinking and creative skills among youth.
